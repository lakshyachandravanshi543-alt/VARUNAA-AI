How Varuna AI Works

Varuna AI bridges the gap between low-cost IoT telemetry and high-grade diagnostics. The engine uses a paired Isolation Forest (for anomaly rejection) and Random Forest Machine Learning model to reconstruct chemical pollution fingerprints.

By mapping non-linear correlations across just 6 basic scalar inputs (pH, DO, Turbidity, Temp, EC, and ORP), the engine achieves 99.50% model validation accuracy without expensive, fragile physical chemical laboratory reagents.
