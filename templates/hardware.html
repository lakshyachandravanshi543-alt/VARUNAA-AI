A highly portable, cost-effective IoT platform engineered for immediate municipal water health analysis.

Processor

THE CORE BRAIN (ESP32 DevKitC v4)

Edge AI Core: Reads raw analog voltages and digital values from all 6 sensors, calibrates them against stored baselines, and pushes secure data via Wi-Fi/HTTP POST to the Flask backend {{ backend_url }}.

Thermal Calibration

DIGITAL VALIDATION (Temperature DS18B20)

Digital Truth: Provides precise temperature data to calibrate all other chemical parameters. Used to validate DO (Dissolved Oxygen) and pH calculations against {{ temperature_range }}.

Clarity

OPTICAL CLARITY (Turbidity Sensor)

Suspended Solids Monitor: Uses optical scattering to measure NTU (gandapan). The backend calls the Weather API to dynamically adjust this alarm threshold if {{ weather_status == 'Rain' }}.

Conductivity

TOTAL DISSOLVED SOLIDS (TDS/EC Sensor)

Dissolved Solids Analyzer: Tracks electrical conductivity (EC). Immediately flags non-visible heavy metals, salts, and chemical runoff.

Acidity

CHEMICAL ACIDITY (pH Sensor Module)

Acidity Metric: The primary monitor for industrial acid waste or agricultural chemical leakage.

Redox Potential

CHEMICAL OXIDATION (Analog ORP Sensor)

Pollutant Oxidizer: Highly sensitive indicator of sewage contamination or specific chemical signatures (like lead/oil).

Power & Chassis

POWER & RUGGEDIZATION (Batteries & Casing)

Portable Edge: Driven by dual 18650 cells for full-shift deployment. The entire system is encased in a 1.5" PVC tube, sealed with IP67-grade epoxy resin.

Anti-Biofouling Management

Automated Sensor Maintenance.

Equipped with automated mechanical wipers and ultrasonic transducers to prevent algal and bacterial buildup. Ensures zero sensor drift and maintains EPA-level accuracy during prolonged remote deployments.

Long-Range Telemetry (LoRaWAN)

Off-Grid Data Transmission.

Upgraded from standard Wi-Fi to integrated LoRaWAN and NB-IoT modules. Capable of securely transmitting 6-dimensional telemetry data up to 15 kilometers without relying on local municipal network infrastructure.
